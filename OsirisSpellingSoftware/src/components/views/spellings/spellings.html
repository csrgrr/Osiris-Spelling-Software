SPELLINGS

The spellings used for the name of Osiris in the Middle Kingdom Coffin Texts can be assigned to 3 different groups according to the main signs that compose them.

  • The second group, dated to the end of the First Intermediate Period, is based on the sign of the flesh (F51) and the mouth (D21).

  • The third group, dated slightly later than the second, would be a combination of the two previous ones or unclassified.

This grouping is a pragmatic and abstract convention that should not be taken as evidence that these groups were created as such in antiquity.
